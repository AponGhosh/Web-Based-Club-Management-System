ULAB Computer Programming Club

ULAB Computer Programming Club (UCPC) is one of the largest club at ULAB. Working since 2004, our main goal is to arrange original and innovative events like hackathons, workshops, training and bootcamps.

About Us


ULAB Computer Programming Club (UCPC) is a student club at University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB). It's made for students who are passionate about computers, high tech, programming, research, design, and anything related to technology. The UCPC has managed to gain top place at ULAB by organizing different Tech events. After organizing the first hackathon and mathematics olympiad, the club is considered as one of the biggest and most active clubs in the ULAB.

Our Events

At UCPC, we organize various and high-quality TECH events, through which we aim to best fit our community needs and to provide a skillful and complete acquirement of knowledge for all the technology enthusiasts!

Our Vision

The UCPC was created in 2004 with one goal: making the student life more dynamic and full of learning, that’s why we organize different types of events like Programming Contest, Workshop, Hackathon, Bootcamp, Seminar, Training Session under several themes. To make it short, we are here to support students during their academic journey by putting new technologies to their benefit and allowing them to exchange their knowledge and experiences. As well as helping them better project themselves in the professional world with good skills.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



How can you join us?

It's quite simple! At the time of pre-registration of each semester, you have to select CCC304 course code. Also, the CCO organizes a club day with the aim of registration those who want to be part of the UCPC adventure and persuading those who still have doubts by presenting the club as well as its history, its departments, members.

Who can join us?

Everyone can do it! You do not have to be a student at ESI, it is enough that you are well motivated and interested in our field.

What is our area of work?

The CSE is mainly oriented towards IT and all that is new technologies, we aim to organize events and Tech projects in any field.

Why join us?

At CSE you will learn and develop leadership skills such as decision making, problem solving, delegation and organization. You will of course acquire skills and experiences which will complement your university course, meet new people and live new experiences. And, of course, have fun!

Do I have to be a CSE member to attend the events?

Not necessarily ! you can be external to the CSE and of course access our events and workshops, you just need to register via the links that we share.

Is it free?

Of course! All of our events are totally free, we are doing this for one ultimate goal which is developing the student's tech community and building the best solutions and projects for our country.
