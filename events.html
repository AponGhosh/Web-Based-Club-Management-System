Our Events

At UCPC, we organize various and high-quality TECH events, through which we aim to best fit our community needs and to provide a skillful and complete acquirement of knowledge for all the technology enthusiasts!

Independence Day Programming Contest


We are thrilled to share that the UCPC hosted a successful Independence Day Programming Contest, in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering on March 23rd. The contest witnessed the participation of some of the brightest minds, showcasing their remarkable programming skills.
Following the contest, a prize-giving ceremony was held to felicitate the deserving winners, while every participant received a certification of participation.

Industry Visit


Industry visit to the Datasoft Manufacturing & Assembly (DMA) Factory at the Bongobondhu Hi-Tech City in Kaliakair, Gazipur on 10 August 2023, Thursday.
A successful industry visit was organized by the ULAB CSE department and UCPC to foster meaningful interactions with industry, knowledge-sharing, and enlighten students on career opportunities. A group of CSE students participated in the visit and gathered knowledge on several ongoing projects in Datasoft. Mr. Hasan Rahman-Ratan, Ex-NASA Scientist and Managing Director of the DMA demonstrated their projects and interacted with the students.

Workshop on Git and GitHub


We are delighted to announce that the ULAB Computer Programming Club-UCPC held a comprehensive workshop on Git and GitHub on 17 August 2023. Thank you for joining us and for your immense support and spontaneous participation, which made this workshop a successful journey. The workshop covered the basics of Git and GitHub, as well as more advanced topics. Our guest speaker, Nure Siddiq, the CEO of bongoDev and Software Engineering Manager at Optimizely, shared his valuable insights with the students.

How To Start Programming


We are thrilled to announce that the Computer Programming Club's (UCPC) event, "How To Start Programming As Fresher" featuring Software Engineer Ahmed Shahriar Tanvir, was a resounding success. Held on October 4th, 2023, the event saw participation from both freshers and other students, along with our respected faculty members.The workshop covered the basics of programming, provided insights into competitive programming, and offered resources and tips for getting started.
An engaging Q&A session allowed attendees to seek answers to their programming questions and refine their ideas. The event concluded with an exciting quiz competition, where participants demonstrated their engagement and tested their newfound knowledge. The winners received exciting prizes, contributing to the event's success

Take-off Programming Contest


We are thrilled to announce that the 'Take-off programming contest, Fall 2023, was a tremendous success for the ULAB Computer Programming Club. We were privileged to have a group of talented and enthusiastic participants who showcased their coding skills and creativity. We extend our heartfelt congratulations to all participants for their hard work and achievements. Each participant received a T-shirt and a certificate of participation in the contest, it was one of the most memorable events ever held at ULAB.
This contest was designed to inspire you to pursue your passion for programming and to foster a culture of continuous learning. We hope that it has served its purpose and that you will join us again in our future events.
